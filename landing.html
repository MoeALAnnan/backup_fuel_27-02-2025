Fuel Precision Pro incorporates a highly precise calculation engine, ensuring accurate fuel quantity indications vital for A320 operations and ground tests.

Fuel Precision Pro was inspired by my experience as a ground test professional at Airbus, where the intricacies of fuel quantity calculations for the A320 became a daily challenge. Juggling day and night shifts, I often found the existing tools lacking in user-friendliness and efficiency. Frustrated by simultaneous editing conflicts and a cumbersome 6 paged Google Sheets calculator, the idea for Fuel Precision Pro was born. It became a personal mission to streamline and enhance the user experience
